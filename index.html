Vicari (170299), Mississippi Reservations

The restaurant is named after a town in the Provence of Palermo in the Italian region Sicily. This town is similar in size to Corinth and has special meaning to the underlying theme of the menu and ambiance of the restaurant. Our intent is for our guests to feel as if they are going to a friend’s place for a great meal. Our team is committed to preparing wonderful food from fresh ingredients and serving you with the utmost care. Join us in one of the most beautiful settings in the region and peruse our vast menu to discover your palate’s delight.


Chef John Mabry

Chef John began cooking at the age of five while standing on a stool in his great aunt’s kitchen. He has had the pleasure of cooking and serving guests for 35 years. His greatest joy is watching a guest take that first bite and their eyes widen and a smile spreads across their face. The chef’s formal training started with the University of Tennessee and then the Hilton training program and finally culinary school in Avignon, France. His second greatest pleasure is teaching/sharing with his culinary team to insure they become the next generation of great culinary artists. If you are in the restaurant and want something specially prepared, just ask. The chef loves to prepare special diets, special preferences or just something special for you.
